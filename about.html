Who I am

I'm AJ, a freelance web designer based in Cebu, Philippines. I have spent the last 7 years helping clients bring ideas to life through strategic planning and user-centered designs — helping brands stand out in the digital era.

My goal is to leave a positive mark and create something remarkable for people I work with. I hear some truly awful stories from client's working with freelancers who can't deliver. It's a big problem. I'm here to do incredible work you want to tell everyone about.

What I do

I help companies from all over the world with tailor-made design solutions. I focus on creating purposeful user interfaces and solutions for the final user that leave a lasting impression. With each project, I push my work to new horizons, always putting quality first.

I work directly with my clients, who normally hire me for: 

Web Design & Development

Digital Design

Project Management

Virtual Business Support

I’m able to work alone on projects as well as collaborate with a team if needed.

What I charge

I work hands-on with projects and limit myself to working with one or two agencies at a time. I want to grow and be invested in the agency/company I work with, to get to know about their systems, needs, habits, and processes better.

With that being said, I regularly choose a full-time role and my services start at $48/hour. However, my rate is negotiable. Feel free to contact me, submit a budget with your request, and I'll let you know if I can work with it.

Getting started

You can reach me by clicking the button below or you can directly shoot me an email at ajvicedor@gmail.com and tell me a little bit about you, your needs, and your project upfront. I will get back to you within the next business day.
