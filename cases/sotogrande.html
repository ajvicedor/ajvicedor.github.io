OVERVIEW

The objective of this project was to create a professional and user-friendly website for the largest privately owned residential development in Andalusia, Spain.

The website will serve as a platform for showcasing properties for sale, and provide a seamless experience for users to browse listings and contact the company for inquiries. The website should also reflect the company's brand identity and values.

SOLUTION

By creating a prototype first in Figma and converting it to WordPress, we were able to build a professional and modern website that enhances the company's online presence, attracts more visitors, and generates leads for property sales.

The website also provides a user-friendly experience for buyers and establishes the company as a reputable and trustworthy player in the real estate market in Andalusia, Spain. Ultimately, the website project helped the company achieve its business objectives.
